Who we are

We Rudra InfoTech (Start Up Registered under MSME, India) engaged in the business of Training, Outsourcing, Sales and Services, Our services including but not limited to Customer Support Executive or Customer Success Specialist (Tele -caller/ on call support).

What we are doing

We depute the personnel with the client on the basis of outsourcing, In the process of outsourcing company can outsource their work to us and the same will done in such a way that it will be more beneficial to the company. We are engaged in the quality work of outsourcing; and we are connecting with clients in such a manner that client’s work will be done in most efficient manner Company’s major object is to focus on providing the best quality of work in timely manner. We are established in Jabalpur (Madhya Pradesh) Tier - 2 city. We are providing work from Jabalpur, and this is cost beneficial to our clients business it helps to save major cost of the company & they get same quality of work like Tier-1 city.

Why you should connect with us

Our clients are getting more benefits then existing market players because we are startup and ensuring that our work quality will remain same throughout the project. We are ensuring you that our cost of the project will be lesser then existing quotations offers to you. We are established startup in Jabalpur; here we can hire graduates and post graduates at less CTC than Tier-1 city offering. Along with this we can hire people who are not willing to move to the tier 1 city as many factors involves including cost of living, and in this you get better resources at less CTC which will be beneficial to the company. We focus on Timely delivery of the Services, to the satisfaction of our clients. We provide the necessary instructions, Trainings to Personnel(s) for performance of Services under the contract.   • All the expenses will be sorted on real time / actual bill cost basis.
  • In the process of outsourcing company can focus on their operations and company can Invest saved money in the research and launch of new features with the cost reduced both in monetary and resource upkeep terms.

How we will work
STRATEGY TO ESTABLISH AND EXPAND:

  • In the initial stage we start with hiring and training 15-20 candidates as per the working policies of your organization after a period of 3 – 4 Months of initial training, we will give these 5-6 Candidates a designation of senior executives.
  • Simultaneously the hiring process of the desired team size will be in process, soon after the initial candidates are ready with the initial training the involvement of your expert executives will be reduced and the training will also be provided by our candidates.
  • A specialized induction of say about 10 days will be conducted, where the experts and experienced employees of your organization provide training and reconcile the checkpoint of training given by our senior employees.
  • Through this arrangement you will always be the guiding partner and you can always overlook whether the process and trainings are going in the same direction as you desire.

What Roles and functions will be provided by us at same charge

  • Customer Support Executive (Tele-caller)
  • Customer Success Specialist (Tele-caller)
  • After execution of contract we will follow the same working model for the CSA (customer support associate) i.e. connecting with client through WhatsApp and Call investment on mobile phones will taken care off by us.
  • At the beginning we will hire minimum 20 people who can take care of the quality and the SLA of the work.
  • We will make people work on the 6 day working model so customers can get resolution on time and we can satisfy the customer by providing the resolution. In this policy your organization will get 1 more working day in a week i.e. 20 working men day in a week and at the end of the year you will get (52x20) = 1040 working men days without any cost.
  • Employees will be getting consecutive week off i.e. 1 day every week and 2.5 holidays in a month.
  • Initially we will follow only day shift working and, as the work will progress we will initiate night shifts also.
  • Our policy related to work timing for day shift will be 9:00 to 6:30.
